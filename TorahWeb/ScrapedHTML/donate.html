Donate Make a Difference

The TorahWeb Foundation, a 501(c)(3) not-for-profit organization (tax ID: 30-0064215), was founded in 1999 at the initiative of members of the community. Its goal is to disseminate divrei Torah and hashkafa, with special attention to contemporary religious and social issues. TorahWeb's board consists of Rav Hershel Schachter, Rav Mayer Twersky, Rav Mordechai Willig, and Rav Michael Rosensweig. The divrei Torah on this site were written by the respective rabbis themselves.

Neither the authors/speakers nor any other individuals involved in TorahWeb earn any money from TorahWeb. In addition, shuls receive the leil iyun programming free of charge. Your donations cover the non-personnel costs incurred by TorahWeb.
